About Me

Backend Developer Puhon!

Greetings, digital adventurers! I'm Gnar, the backend ninja with a knack for making code as fun as a barrel of monkeys (and about as unpredictable). Picture a mischievous imp with a keyboard and a penchant for puns, and you've got a pretty good idea of who you're dealing with here. I'm fluent in the languages of servers, databases, and APIs, but my real superpower lies in making technology feel less like rocket science and more like a friendly game of Twister. Need a chuckle while we refactor that spaghetti code? I've got puns for days, my friend. When I'm not busy slaying bugs or cracking jokes, you can find me at the local comedy club, honing my craft as a part-time stand-up comedian. Who says developers can't moonlight as entertainers? Life's too short for boring code and serious faces!
